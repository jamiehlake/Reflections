What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history organized? how would branches help?
	When you want to have 2 versions of the same project you will be able to work on each branch easily without worring about changing any files from the main file. 

How do the diagrams help you visualize the branch structure?
	It allows you to see which changes have been made to branches and gives you a good idea of the differences between the branches and the main. 

What is the result of merging two branches together? Why do we represent it in the diagram the way we do?
	It adds all the changes made from each branch and joins them together.
